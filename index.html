About Us

At EcoFil, we bring Filipino heritage to life, one stitch at a time. Based in the historic city of Vigan, Ilocos Sur, our enterprise celebrates tradition and sustainability by offering modernized rentals of Filipino cultural attire made from katcha fabric and embellished with elegant inabel weaving. We believe that fashion should tell a story and serve a purpose. That’s why each piece in our collection is thoughtfully designed to honor our past while suiting the comfort and aesthetics of today’s generation. Whether you're a local reconnecting with your roots or a tourist seeking an authentic cultural experience, EcoFil allows you to wear history and feel Filipino—literally and emotionally. But EcoFil is more than just fashion. Our platform offers tech-enabled experiences, including online booking, AI-powered 3D virtual try-ons, and curated heritage-inspired photoshoots. You can even personalize your look with optional hair and makeup services and mix-and-match accessories. Every rental supports our mission: to empower local weavers, preserve Filipino artistry, and share the richness of our culture in a way that's livable, shareable, and unforgettable.

Our Weavers

Meet the Hands Behind the Threads

Nanay Lucia

Vigan, Ilocos Sur

“Each piece I make is like a prayer—woven with patience and pride.”

A master weaver and community icon preserving the elegance of Ilocos inabel weaving. Lucia is a guardian of tradition, ensuring cultural identity remains vibrant through her art.

Manang Pilar

Bantay, Ilocos Sur

“When someone wears inabel, they wear my family’s legacy.”

With over 50 years of weaving experience, Pilar is a living legacy. Her passion for inabel weaving is passed down to generations, creating a lasting impact on Ilocano heritage.

Ellaine

Santa, Ilocos Sur

“I want to keep weaving relevant for my generation.”

Ellaine brings youth and modern vision into traditional weaving. Each rental supports artisans like her and preserves centuries-old craftsmanship for future generations.
