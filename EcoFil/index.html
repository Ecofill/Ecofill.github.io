About Us

At EcoFil, we bring Filipino heritage to life, one stitch at a time. Based in the historic city of Vigan, Ilocos Sur, our enterprise celebrates tradition and sustainability by offering modernized rentals of Filipino cultural attire made from katcha fabric and adorned with inabel weaving.
